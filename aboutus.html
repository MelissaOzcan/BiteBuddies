About Us

Our Mission

At Bite Buddies, our mission is to provide a helping hand to students in making delicious and nutritious meals that suit their dietary restrictions and budget. We believe that everyone deserves access to tasty and affordable recipes.

How It Works

Bite Buddies simplifies the meal planning process. Users can select their dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, kosher, pescetarian, or none) and their budget range. Our platform then suggests recipes tailored to these preferences, ensuring a diverse range of options for every user.

Our Team

Bite Buddies was created by a group of Stevens students who noticed that they were struggling balancing their nutrition and their busy academics. They wanted an easy and quick way to find healthy recipes that worked for them and knew could help others!
